@@ Satchel of Gold v1.0
@@ A wallet object each player carries for the custom money system in use.
@@ This is used in conjunction with the global bank described in "bank.txt".

@@ Created and tested under PennMUSH v1.8.7

@create Satchel of gold
@set Satchel of gold=QUIET
@set Satchel of gold=MONITOR
@set Satchel of gold=!NO_COMMAND
@lock/give Satchel of gold==#1
@lock/drop Satchel of gold==#1
@lock/enter Satchel of gold==#1

&AMOUNT Satchel of gold=50
&DESCRIBE Satchel of gold=A soft brown bag with a golden drawstring. Type [ansi(h,gold)] to see its contents.
&USE Satchel of gold=You cautiously check to see that your bag hasn't disappeared.
&GIVE_LOCK`FAILURE Satchel of gold=That type of charity must come later.
&DROP_LOCK`FAILURE Satchel of gold=That type of detachment must come later.
&GET_AMOUNT Satchel of gold=switch(v(amount),1,v(amount) piece,v(amount) pieces)
&STATUS Satchel of gold=pemit(loc(me),You have [u(get_amount)] of gold.)
&CMD_GOLD Satchel of gold=$gold:think u(status)
&ADESCRIBE Satchel of gold=think u(status)
